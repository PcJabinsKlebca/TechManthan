About Tech-Manthan

As the legacy of KLE BCA Hubli continues the in- house college fest is hosted and organised by the BCA lV sem students for lst and final year students on 27th and 28th may the theme is samudra manthan followed with other sub-themes. Click On More Info For Brief Details.

Brief Info:
As the legacy of "KLE's BCA Huballi" continues, the in house college fest "Tech Manthan" is hosted and organized by the BCA fourth semester students for first and final year students on 27th and 28th May 2022. This event is basically conducted to enhance technical skills of students and infuse them with technical knowledge and boost their knowledge with practicality. Through this event, the participants glean technical knowledge which is vestibule for their technical career. The theme is "Samudra Manthan" followed by other sub-themes.. Those are :

  • YUGANTARA (Coding )
  • KAUSTUBHA (Communication)
  • PRASHNOTTARA (Quiz)
  • SHARANGA (Software Testing )
  • VASUKI (Ice-breaker)
  • INDRAANUJA (Tech-lead)
  • SAMMOHINI ( Photography and web designing)
  • KSHEERASAGARA (Treasure hunt)

The battle is between intellectuality and technology on two days where victorious one will be crowned with General Championship.
